What Is Steam StoRox?

Steam StoRox is a host page for the Father Ryan Arts Center Coding Club.

With funding from the My Brothers Keeper Grant from the Sprout Fund, in partnership with several local volunteers, the Father Ryan Arts Center Coding Club originated as an initiative to teach teens the basics of programming and building websites by teaching the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

The following pages were designed by coding club participants and serve a dual purpose of resume/portfolio as well as an active website they can share with their friends/peers.
